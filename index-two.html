How to make Spaghetti & Meatball

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons chopped basil
  • 3 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1½ pounds ground ground beef
  • ¾ cup dried Italian style bread crumbs ( Progresso)
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • Large jar good quality Marinara sauce (such as Rao's)
  • 1 pound spaghetti

How to make Spaghetti

Put you're spaghetti into a boiling pot of water, after a while, when it's soft, you sieve it and rinse the spaghetti to drain the water and also remove impurities/unwanted particles.

how to make the meatballs

In a large bowl, whisk together the egg, basil, parsley, oregano, salt, pepper, garlic and water, then add the meat, then the breadcrumbs and cheese and mix until just combined and then roll them into a small meatballs and place them into a baking sheet. cook the meatballs for like 10 mins and flip them then cook them for another 10 mins or until they are brown then put the marinara on the simmer and put the meatballs in there aswell and cover it with a lid for like 10 mins or until the meatballs are cooked. and you know how to make spagathetti.
