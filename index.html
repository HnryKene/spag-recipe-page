Looking for a spaghetti to try?

Why not try spaghetti and meatball?

Spaghetti and meatballs is a classic Italian-American dish featuring spaghetti pasta served with savory meatballs in a rich tomato sauce. The meatballs are typically made from ground beef, pork, or a mixture of both, combined with breadcrumbs, eggs, garlic, and Italian herbs. These meatballs are browned and then simmered in a homemade or store-bought tomato sauce, often seasoned with garlic, onions, basil, and oregano. The spaghetti is cooked al dente, then tossed in the sauce and served with the meatballs on top. The dish is often garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
